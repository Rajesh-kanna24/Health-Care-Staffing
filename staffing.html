PEDIATRIC CARE

Pediatric Skilled & Non-Skilled Care

Trinity Life Care provides compassionate in-home care for children with medical and developmental needs.

Skilled Pediatric Services:

  • Pediatric skilled nursing
  • Medication administration
  • Chronic condition management (asthma, diabetes, congenital conditions)
  • Post-hospital pediatric recovery care
  • Respiratory support monitoring

Non-Skilled Pediatric Care:

  • Personal care assistance
  • Feeding and mobility support
  • Developmental support assistance
  • Family respite care
  • Behavioral and routine support
