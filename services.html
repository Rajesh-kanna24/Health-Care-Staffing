Payment & Coverage Options

Flexible Options Tailored
to Your Needs.

Trinity Life Care offers flexible payment options tailored to individual needs, eligibility, and services provided.

Private Pay
  • Hourly, overnight, and live-in care
  • Short-term and long-term support
  • Transparent pricing with customized care plans
Medicare (Eligible Skilled Services)

Coverage may apply when:

  • Services are physician-ordered
  • Delivered under an approved care plan
  • Home health eligibility criteria are met

Covered services may include:

  • Skilled nursing
  • Therapy services
  • Post-hospital recovery care
Commercial Insurance
  • Coverage varies by plan
  • Prior authorization may be required
  • Insurance verification assistance provided
Long-Term Care Insurance
  • May cover non-skilled services
  • Eligibility based on policy terms
  • Claims and documentation support available
Medicaid Waiver Programs (When Applicable)
  • May cover personal care services
  • Eligibility determined by program guidelines
  • Enrollment assistance provided (when applicable)
Veterans Benefits (When Applicable)
  • VA Aid & Attendance programs
  • Support for personal care services
  • Guidance on available resources
Our Approach

Patient-Centered
Care Philosophy

At Trinity Life Care, every decision begins with the patient. Each care plan is tailored to the patient’s medical, emotional, cultural, and personal needs.

We believe care should be:

  • Individualized, not standardized
  • Compassionate, not transactional
  • Coordinated, not fragmented
Our Priorities
Preserving dignity and independence
Maintaining clear communication with families
Ensuring physician collaboration and oversight
Delivering continuity across care settings

"Our goal is simple: improve outcomes while enhancing quality of life."

Diversity & Inclusion Commitment

We believe healthcare should reflect the communities it serves.
Our commitment includes:

  • Building a diverse and inclusive workforce
  • Promoting equal opportunity in hiring and leadership
  • Delivering culturally competent care
  • Maintaining a discrimination-free environment

Diversity strengthens our perspective, improves communication, and builds trust with those we serve.
