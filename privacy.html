HIPAA

Notice of privacy practices

Trinity Life Care respects the privacy of our patients and complies with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA).

Use & disclosure of health information

We may use and disclose protected health information (PHI) for:

  • Treatment and care coordination
  • Coordination with physicians, specialists, and other providers involved in your care
  • Payment and billing activities
  • Healthcare operations and quality improvement
  • Public health reporting when required by law
  • Other uses authorized by the patient or permitted under applicable law

Patient rights include

  • The right to access and obtain copies of medical records
  • The right to request amendments to medical information
  • The right to request restrictions on certain disclosures
  • The right to receive confidential communications
  • The right to receive an accounting of disclosures
  • The right to file a privacy complaint without fear of retaliation
  • The right to receive a paper copy of this Notice of Privacy Practices

To request access to records or file a privacy complaint, contact Trinity Life Care on the phone number or email published on this website.

Website privacy policy

Information collected through this website may include:

  • Contact form submissions
  • Employment applications
  • Referral requests
  • Website usage analytics
  • Cookies and related technologies

Trinity Life Care does not sell personal information. Information collected through our website is used solely for communication, service delivery, recruitment, operational purposes, and legal compliance.

We are committed to safeguarding the information you share with us by:

  • Applying reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards
  • Limiting access to authorized personnel only
  • Retaining information only as long as necessary or required by law
  • Honoring requests to access, correct, or delete your information where applicable

Terms of use

Information presented on this website is intended for informational purposes only and does not establish a provider-patient relationship. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Medical emergencies should be directed to 911 or the nearest emergency department.

By accessing or using this website, you agree that:

  • Content may be updated, revised, or removed at any time without prior notice
  • You will not misuse, copy, distribute, or reproduce website content without written permission
  • All trademarks, logos, and materials remain the property of Trinity Life Care
  • Any external links are provided for convenience and do not imply endorsement

Your continued use of this website constitutes acceptance of these terms.

Non-discrimination policy

Trinity Life Care complies with applicable federal and state civil rights laws and does not discriminate based on:

  • Race
  • Color
  • National origin
  • Age
  • Disability
  • Sex
  • Religion
  • Veteran status
  • Any other protected classification under applicable law

Language assistance and reasonable accommodations are available upon request.

A two-way partnership

Patient rights & responsibilities

Patients have the right to

  • Be treated with dignity and respect
  • Participate in care planning
  • Receive information regarding services and treatment options
  • Voice complaints without fear of retaliation
  • Privacy and confidentiality of health information

Patients are responsible for

  • Providing accurate health information
  • Participating in their care plan
  • Following the agreed plan of care and safety instructions
  • Reporting concerns or changes in condition promptly
  • Respecting staff and caregivers

Corporate compliance & ethics

Trinity Life Care maintains a corporate compliance program designed to promote ethical conduct and adherence to all applicable federal and state laws. Our compliance program includes:

  • HIPAA compliance
  • Fraud, waste, and abuse prevention
  • Employee training and education
  • Internal reporting mechanisms
  • Quality assurance and performance improvement

This program applies to all employees, contractors, and business partners, and is reviewed regularly to ensure ongoing effectiveness.

Reporting compliance concerns

Employees, patients, families, referral sources, and business partners may report compliance concerns confidentially. Reports may include:

  • Privacy violations
  • Billing concerns
  • Fraud or abuse concerns
  • Workplace misconduct
  • Quality of care concerns

Retaliation against any individual who reports concerns in good faith is strictly prohibited.

Accessibility statement

Trinity Life Care is committed to making our website accessible to all users, including individuals with disabilities.

Our ongoing accessibility efforts include:

  • Following recognized web accessibility standards and best practices
  • Supporting keyboard navigation and screen-reader compatibility
  • Maintaining clear text, readable layouts, and sufficient color contrast
  • Reviewing and improving accessibility on an ongoing basis

If you encounter accessibility barriers while using this website, please contact us so that we may provide assistance and improve accessibility.

Employment applicant privacy

Information submitted through employment applications is used solely for recruitment, credentialing, onboarding, employment verification, and compliance purposes.

We handle applicant information responsibly by ensuring:

  • Data is collected only for legitimate hiring and credentialing needs
  • Information is maintained securely and accessed only by authorized personnel
  • Records are retained in line with applicable employment and regulatory requirements
  • Applicant information is never sold or used for marketing purposes

Applicants may contact us with any questions about how their information is handled.

SMS communication consent

By providing a mobile phone number, patients, clients, employees, and applicants consent to receive communications from Trinity Life Care regarding:

  • Appointment reminders
  • Scheduling updates
  • Staffing assignments
  • Care coordination notifications
  • Administrative communications

Message and data rates may apply. Consent may be revoked at any time by contacting Trinity Life Care.
